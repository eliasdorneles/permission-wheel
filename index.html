Permission Wheel

The permission wheel is a tool conceived by Gysa Jaoui to represent graphically the main limits of a person's life script, it is described by Laurie Hawkes in the article The Permission Wheel, published in Transactional Analysis Journal, Vol 37, No. 2, July 2007. The article is also available on Laurie Hawkes' website.

This is a work-in-progress online version made by Elias Dorneles.
